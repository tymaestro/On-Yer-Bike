About Us

Welcome to On Yer Bike! We are a Dublin-based duo who have combined our passion for bicycles and our love of exchanging ideas to create something special for the whole community. Our goal is to make cycling more accessible and affordable for everyone. At On Yer Bike, we sell bicycles that have been reconditioned by our team of volunteers, many of whom have been trained in bike maintenance through our workshop program.


Whether you're browsing for a bike, looking to learn a new skill at one of our workshops or just looking to meet a friendly face, we will always be there for you on what we hope is the beginning of many cycling adventures!
