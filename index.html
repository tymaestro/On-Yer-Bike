About Us

On Yer Bike is a Dublin-based bicycle website that aims to make cycling more accessible and affordable to everyone. We sell both new and second-hand bikes and also offer workshops for bike maintenance so you can take care of your own bike on a regular basis. This is also a wonderful opportunity to both learn a new skill and be a part of the local community. It is our goal to provide a friendly environment that caters to cyclists of all levels.
